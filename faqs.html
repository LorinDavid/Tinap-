    Frequently Asked Questions

    • We offer a variety of freshly bakes pastries such as cupcakes, croissants, bread and more.

    • Yes, all our pastries are baked fresh every day.

      How should I store my pastries? ▶

    • Keep them in a cool, dry place or refrigerate if needed to maintain freshness.

      How long do pastries stay fresh? ▶

    • Most pastries are best consumed within 1 to 3 days.
