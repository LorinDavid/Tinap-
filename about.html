    ABOUT US

    Five students. One cramped kitchen. A shared delusion that we could make this work.

    We didn't have investors. We had savings accounts we drained and credit cards we maxed. Aguas Kim, Ayeras Macraine, David Cara Lorin, Delfina Francine Jayde, and Espino Justin Rain—we met in PSAU, bonded over burnt brioche coding, and decided to bet on ourselves. That was years ago.

    The first six months? We were open 14 hours a day, sleeping in shifts, and eating our own mistakes because we couldn't afford to waste ingredients.

    OUR MISSION

    To still be standing next year. To make something good enough that you come back.

    To craft exceptional baked goods that honor tradition, celebrate quality, and create meaningful connections—one customer, one bite, one morning at a time.

    OUR BRAND VALUES

    We Show Up

    Even when the dough doesn't prove, the supplier is late, and someone calls in sick. Especially then.

    We Adapt

    Our menu changes with what we can actually source, what actually sells, and what we can physically produce with five people and two ovens.

    We Waste Nothing

    Not because it's trendy, but because margins are thin and every egg counts.
